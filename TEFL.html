What Is TEFL and What Is TEFL Certification?

TEFL (Teaching English as a Foreign Language) refers to teaching English to non-native speakers in countries where English is not the primary language. A TEFL certification is a professional qualification that provides the skills and knowledge needed to teach English effectively in classrooms worldwide.

Other similar certifications include:
