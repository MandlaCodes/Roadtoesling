Top Websites for ESL Teachers

  • Breaking News English – Free news-based ESL lessons with varying difficulty levels.
  • ESL Library – Printable lesson plans and worksheets (paid subscription).
  • BBC Learning English – High-quality English learning resources for students.
  • Ellii (formerly ESL Library) – Engaging lesson plans for various levels.
  • ISLCollective – Free worksheets, PowerPoints, and video lessons.
  • Teach This – Communicative ESL activities and worksheets.
  • Dave’s ESL Café – ESL job board and teacher forum.
  • Games4ESL – Free ESL games, worksheets, and activities.
  • Tay’s Teaching Toolkit – Engaging games and resources for young learners.
  • Korshare – Free shared ESL teaching resources.
  • ESL Authority – A collection of free ESL lesson plans.
  • ESL KidStuff – ESL lesson plans and teaching materials for kids.
  • Teaching English (British Council) – Free resources and professional development for ESL teachers.
  • English 4 Kids – Printable ESL lessons for young learners.
  • Twinkl – A treasure trove of ESL lesson plans, worksheets, and printable resources for all ages.

Useful Tools for ESL Teachers

  • Canva – Create engaging worksheets, slides, and posters.
  • Kahoot! – Interactive quizzes for student engagement.
  • Quizlet – Flashcards and games for vocabulary practice.
  • Wordwall – Customizable games and activities.
  • Grammarly – Grammar and spell-checking tool.
  • Zoom – Essential for online ESL teaching.
  • Google Classroom – Organize assignments and lessons.
  • Baamboozle – Fun classroom games and quizzes.

YouTube Quiz Games for ESL

  • Games4ESL – Interactive ESL games, quizzes, and activities for students.
  • Quiz Empire – A variety of quiz game formats that cover different aspects of language learning.
  • Quiz Blitz – A YouTube channel with fast-paced quiz games for vocabulary and language review.
